Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I convert a disk image to a different disk image format?

A: Open the disk image, select in the archive tree, and then click the Save As Disk Image button. Choose the desired format.

Q: Why is my random-access DOS text file truncated? Why can't I view an Applesoft program on a DOS 3.3 disk?

A: Check the "raw" setting. It must be enabled when viewing random-access text files, and disabled when viewing BASIC programs.
